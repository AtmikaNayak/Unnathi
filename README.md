## Unnathi
Unnathi is a web platform designed to help women discover scholarships, government schemes, internships, and personal finance resources.
The platform also includes an AI assistant called HerBot that helps users quickly find relevant opportunities and guidance.
---
### Key Features
🎓 Scholarship Discovery
Users can browse and search for scholarships available for women and students. This helps them quickly find educational funding opportunities they may not have known about.
🏛 Government Schemes
The platform lists government programs that support women’s education, entrepreneurship, and financial independence.
💼 Internship Opportunities
Students and young professionals can explore internship opportunities that help them gain real-world experience and build their careers.
💰 MoneyLab
A learning section that introduces users to personal finance concepts such as saving, budgeting, and basic investing.
